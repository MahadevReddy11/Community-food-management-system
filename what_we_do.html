What We Do

We bridge the gap between food donors and volunteers to fight food wastage.

Our Mission

Every day, tons of edible food go to waste while thousands go hungry. Our mission is to connect communities by redistributing surplus food from donors to those who need it the most through the help of volunteers.

How It Works

1. Donors

Restaurants, households, and event organizers post their excess food with details.

2. Volunteers

Local volunteers view available food posts and pick up the donations.

3. Community

The collected food is shared with people in need, reducing both hunger and waste.
